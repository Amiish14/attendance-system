Category guide (Minimum Wages Act)
  • Unskilled — no formal training (helpers, loaders, sweepers).
  • Semi-Skilled — minor training; carries some responsibility (packers, machine attendants).
  • Skilled — qualified trade with technical know-how (forklift operators, electricians).
  • Highly Skilled — substantial training + supervisory ability (foreman, shift in-charge).
